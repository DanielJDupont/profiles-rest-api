The django admin is an adminsitrative tool that allows you to easily manage your database, at least 
the models you have registered to your database.

It is easy to inspect the database with it, check your models, and modify data.

Note that django admin needs to have the models it will display registered with it.
Our UserProfile model is registered with admin.py.
